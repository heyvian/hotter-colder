Find the Doughnut

Multiplayer WebXR Experiment

It looks like your device is not supported. You'll need a modern Android phone and Chrome or a modern iPhone with WebXR Viewer by Mozilla.

One person uses this device to hide the doughnut and then the next player uses this device to try and find it with 'warmer' and 'colder' cues.

This is very experimental and it's reliability leaves much to be desired.
